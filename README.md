# Cuenta-Wallet-Weekly-Snapshot

Generate the DiDi Cuenta CX Executive Weekly Review for Week XX.

Inputs:

1. Weekly KPI Dashboard
- Contact Volume
- CSAT
- Recontact
- TPO
- FCR
- Pickup Rate
- SLA
- AHT
- Open Tickets
- Backlog

2. Top Contact Reasons
(Include ticket volume and WoW comparison)

3. CSAT by Contact Reason

4. Recontact Analysis

5. Product & Operations Updates

6. Incidents (if any)

7. Strategic Initiatives / Project Updates

Requirements:

• Follow the standard DiDi Cuenta CX Executive Weekly Review structure.
• Analyze the information instead of summarizing it.
• Explain what happened, why it happened, customer impact, business impact, operational implications, and next steps.
• Compare against the previous week whenever data is available.
• Do not invent information or metrics.
• Keep the writing concise, executive, and business-oriented.
